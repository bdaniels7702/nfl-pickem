How scoring works

  • Each game you pick correctly is worth 10 points
  • Nail every game in a week for a perfect week bonus of 100 points
  • Points are awarded automatically once results are posted for a week

Picks lock at kickoff

  • Each game locks automatically at its real kickoff time — you can't change a pick once that game starts.
  • Games you haven't picked yet stay open until their own kickoff, so you can keep editing the rest of the week.
  • Picks auto-save as you tap, and Submit week locks an entire week early if you want.

Tiebreaker

  • Each week, predict the combined final score of the Monday night game.
  • If two players are tied on points, the one whose guesses are closest to the actual MNF totals ranks higher.
  • The tiebreaker input locks when Monday night kicks off.

Odds & autopick

  • When the commissioner posts odds, each game shows the favorite, spread, over/under, and money lines.
  • ⚡ Autopick favorites fills your open games with the favored team — and the home team for any game without odds yet.

Team records

  • The Records tab tracks two things per team: each team's actual win–loss from posted results, and your own record when you back that team.

Pools & friends

  • Create a pool to get a shareable code and invite link; everyone who joins appears under "My pool" in the standings.
  • Search for people by name or email, send friend requests, and filter the standings to just your friends.
  • Your account is saved to the cloud, so you can sign in from any device and pick up where you left off.
